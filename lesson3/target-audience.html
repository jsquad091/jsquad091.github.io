Target Audience:

People who want to know what the weather is going to be like in the future as well as the present. People who want to know specific numbers such as humitidy, temperature, and probability of percipitaion. People who are planning a trip and want to know how the weather is going to be like at their destination. Generally people who come and visit this site are people who are going to be searching for information about the weather.
