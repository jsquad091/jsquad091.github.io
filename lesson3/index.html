Site Name:

The Daily Temperature

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to provide real time weather updates and information to people that visit the website. The website will provide current weather in all areas of the world as well as accurate future weather predictions.The website will be simple to use and the knowledge gained from visiting the site will leave the visitor feeling more intelligent than they felt before they visited the site. Those who visit the site will be able to sign up for notifications on either their desktop or other internet connected devices if they wish to receive daily or weekly updates about the weather in thier area.

Domain:

www.TheDailyTempurature.com

Advantages

The name has a popular ending (.com).
This name is easy to pronouse and type when heard.

Considerations

People might misspell temperature when they are typing this name.
The longer the name, the harder it is to remember.
